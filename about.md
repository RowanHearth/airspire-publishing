<h2 id="rowanhearth">Rowan Hearth</h2>
<p style="font-size:small">Author of <i><a href="series.html#mistworld">Mistworld</a></i></p>
<p>Rowan is a first-time author living in Kansas City with his family. A lifelong lover of fantasy and science fiction, Rowan believes the best stories revolve around unforgettable characters in unforgettable places.</p>
<p>The idea for the <i>Mistworld</i> series was born during an offhand conversation about crafting compelling three-dimensional characters. While illustrating how realistic characters grow from the world around them, Rowan accidentally conjured the world, conflict and heroine of <i>Mistbound</i> on the spot. By the end of the conversation, it was clear: this world needed to be written into existence.</p>
<p>When not building fictional realms, Rowan can usually be found at home, reading or working at his day job as a software engineer.</p>
